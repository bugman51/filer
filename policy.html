Privacy & Usage Policy

Your trust is our foundation. Our commitment is your privacy.

Data Access & Integrity

WiXnation strictly utilizes **publicly available football data** from trusted sources. We do not engage in data harvesting that violates terms of service or infringe upon proprietary rights. Our mission is to democratize access to sports data while remaining compliant and ethical in our approach.

User Privacy

We do not collect personal data. Users are not required to register, log in, or submit any identifiable information. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any surveillance technologies. Your interaction with WiXnation is entirely anonymous and secure.

No Guarantees Disclaimer

All analytical outputs — including predictive insights such as Over/Under goals and Win Strength — are **informational tools only**. WiXnation does not guarantee accuracy, betting outcomes, or financial gain. Users are advised to combine our insights with their own independent analysis.

Open Source Transparency

Our scraping engine is publicly available via GitHub. This ensures full **transparency and auditability**. Users can inspect the codebase, understand how data is fetched, and even contribute to improvements. We encourage a community of collaboration and integrity.

Responsible Usage

Users are responsible for how they utilize the data provided by WiXnation. We advocate for **responsible betting** and do not endorse gambling practices that are unethical, illegal, or harmful. Always adhere to your local laws and regulations.
